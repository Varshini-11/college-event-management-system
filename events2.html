Poster Presentation

A poster presentation is a concise and visual way to communicate research findings or information. It typically consists of a poster board displaying key points, graphics,and text that summarize the main aspects of a project or study. Poster presentations are commonly used in academic conferences, allowing presenters to engage with their audience and convey complex ideas in a clear and accessible format. Effective posters capture attention, convey information succinctly, and encourage discussions with viewers.
